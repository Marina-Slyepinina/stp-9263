How to Play

  • 1. Start Your Quest:

    Take on the role of the Queen and begin your epic journey.

  • 2. Explore Realms:

    Travel through magical forests, mountain paths, ruins, and more.

  • 3. Solve Puzzles:

    Unlock gates, decode glyphs, and reveal ancient truths.

  • 4. Manage Resources:

    Collect herbs, crystals, and artifacts to aid in quests.

  • 5. Rebuild the Kingdom:

    Complete missions to upgrade your palace and secure your people’s future.

  • 6. Make Wise Decisions:

    Choose your words and allies carefully — they shape your story.
